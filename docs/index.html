IoT Plug and play FAQs
Q. What is IoT Plug and Play?
A. It is an open modeling language that allows device builders and solution builders to connect IoT devices to the cloud without writing a single line of embedded code.
Q. How does it work?
A. The open modeling language allows IoT devices to declare their capabilities to the cloud, which can then automatically understand the dive and start interacting with it, without writing any code.
Q. How much does it cost?
A. IoT Plug and Play is free.
Q. Where can customers and solution builders find IoT devices that are IoT Plug and Play enabled?
A. They can find devices at Certified for IoT device catalog
Q. How can device manufactures and device builders certify their devices for IoT Plug and Play?
A. They can follow this tutorial to certify their devices.
